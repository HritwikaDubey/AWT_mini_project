Restaurants

Khao Galli In Kothrud Is Every Foodie's Dream Come True

The Coriander Leaf

The Coriander Leaf is a bar and a casual dining place in Kothrud, West Pune. The place has an ornate ambiance along with a beautiful view of the city. Apparently, this is the place where you could indulge in warm conversations over cold beer with a loved one or friends. They serve delicious dishes of the North Indian, Chinese, Seafood and Continental cuisines here. Every now and then, live entertainment shows are organized here and no matter how busy the place gets, the services are prompt and the staff is courteous. They also make arrangements for all kinds of functions and gatherings and also have a private dining area available.

Momo Lover

For every Momo lover this is the place is a must go, with wide range of momo cafes you will be everytime guessing which place to go. Momo outlet is already a crowd favourite, with youngsters flocking here every evening. You’ll be served everything you’ll find at any streetside momo thela, only a little bit more hygienic. Steamed, fried or tandoori; get your momos the way you like them or if you want something different, try their Malaysian or cheese momos.

Cafe Durga

Kothrud and Cafe Durga are synonymous with each other and anyone who visits the area has to try out this iconic place. Students, old-folks, working professionals, middle-aged crowd, everyone seems to love Cafe Durga and its coffee. You’re not a true-blue Puneri if you haven’t sampled Durga’s cold coffee. With people making the food pilgrimage from all around the city to sample the best, Durga’s kuka shouldn’t be missed out on.

The Inexpensive Chinese

The spicy, tangy and gravy-based Chinese food is an absolute favourite go-out-to-eat. A typical Chinese meal will have two things - a carbohydrate or starch like noodles, rice or buns, and accompanying stir fries or dishes of veggies.That are absolutely irresistible.

The South Indian

No food list is complete without the mention of some delicious idlis and dosas, and hence, We love them for their South Indian specialities including Kotte Idli, Tatte Idli, Appe, Mangalore Buns, Neer Dosa and Appams; not to mention Lemon Rice and Bisi Bele Bhat.

Waari Book Cafe

Waari Cafe is Kothrud is the cafe you’ve been looking for. Replete with books, a pleasant decor and a small menu of munchies, you’d want to camp here for hours! a really good book café that has a good collection of books, some simple coffee and chaai on the menu along with sandwiches and basic grub to keep you replenished while you devour your favourite book. There are around 3,000 books at the cafe, almost all of them fiction.

Sujata Mastani
