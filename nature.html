Jog, Walk Or Stroll On The Tekdi


Vetal Tekdi


The Tekdis (hillocks) of Pune are the lungs of the city. The tekdi in Kothrud is approachable by a short climb or by road. It is the perfect place for your morning or evening jog or walk. You can either hike up from the foothills from MIT College base or take your vehicle up through the road which goes to the ARAI (Automotive Research Association of India). Once there you can choose a path. These paths will eventually lead you to various places in the city like Law College Road via the Maruti Mandir, Pashan via the abandoned quarry, Senapati Bapat Road via the Aapla Maruti Mandir & Vetal Tekdi.


Arai Tekdi aka Vetal tekdi is the highest point in the city of Pune, from here you can see the horizon and the cityscape. You can watch plenty of rare birds in their natural habitats, jog, meditate and more.
