CityPride Kothrud
Entertainment for everyone

It is the best place to check out all the latest movies in the city for cinemagoers. City Pride Multiplex is cinema theatre located in the residential area of Kothrud. It showcases Marathi, Hindi and English movies. After renovations 4 more screens are added, hence more movie timings and more options.
